Thirunallar

It is small town in Karaikal, Puducherry.

The place is famous for the shrine of Lord Sani (Saturn), Tirunallar Saneeswaran Temple within the temple dedicated to Lord Dharbharanyeswaran, a form of Lord Shiva.

It has numerous sacred bathing places called the ‘Theertams’ of which the ‘Nala theertam’ is the most important one. It is believed that by bathing here, one washes off all kinds of misfortunes and afflictions caused by one's past of karmas.

Thirunallar Saneeswaran Temple

Thirunallar Saniswaran Temple or Dharbaranyeswarar Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Sani( Saturn).

The presiding deity is Lord Siva, Dharbaranyeswar and the place Thirunallar was historically called Dharbaranyam.

Prayers are also offered to Saneeswarar, originally treated as the door keeper of the shrine.

The tradition is to worship Saneeswarar before entering the inner sanctum of Lord Siva.

 

Badrakaliyamman Temple

It is located in the Ambagarathur village of the Karaikal district.

The ancient temple is dedicated to Goddess Parasakthi, an incarnation of Goddess Parvathi who was sent by Lord Shiva to save the people from the two demons Ambaran and Amban.

The temple houses an image of the goddess facing north with 4 arms made using terracotta.


 

Karaikal Beach

It is the popular beach in the coastal area of Karaikal city.

This sandy beach is visited by many tourists.

Fountains, Children’s Park, Beach Restaurant, Tennis Court are the other places of attraction here.
