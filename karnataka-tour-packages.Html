Karnataka

It is a state in the south west region of India with its coastlines at the Arabian Sea. Karnataka is a place where vibrant worlds such as the misty hill stations, lush green forests, roaring waterfalls, ancient sculptured temples, modern cities and beaches seamlessly come across one another every few hundred kilometres.

Coorg –

Kodagu or Coorg famously known as“The Scotland of India” is the most affluent hill station in Karnataka with breathtaking scenery, lush teakwood and sandalwood forest, and acres of tea and coffee plantation.

This place is ideal for outdoor activities like trekking, angling, white water rafting etc.....

Major attractions: Abbey Falls, Talacauvery, Nagarahole National Park, Tibetan Buddhist Golden temple, Madikeri etc....

 

Mysore –

It is the third largest, flamboyant city in Karnataka with exceptional weather throughout the year.

It is an important centre of rich heritage, art and culture hence called as the Palace City of India. From palaces and ornate temples to gardens and galleries, there are multiple attractions here to encounter.

Major attractions: : Mysore Palace, Mysore Zoo, Brindavan Gardens, Chamundi Hills, Rail Museum, Somnathpur temple etc....


 

Bangalore –

This highly developed cosmopolitan city reflects a blend of old and new fashion.

Lined with long stretches of trees and parks, famous heritage buildings, busy markets and newly constructed buildings, Bangalore seems to have a bit of everything for everyone.

Also coined as “Silicon Valley of India”

Major attractions:Bannerghatta National Park, Lalbagh Botanical Garden, Wonder La Amusement Park, Commercial Street, Tipu Sultan’s Palace, Iskon Temple etc....

 

Hampi –

It is a fantasy world of rocks and ruins located within the city of Vijayanagara, the former capital of the Vijayanagara Empire. Hampi is recognised as a UNESCO World Heritage Site, owing to its ancient temples, forts and other monuments.

Major attractions: Virupaksha Temple, Vithala Temple, Hampi Stables, Queen’s bath, Hampi Bazaar, Nandhi Statue etc....

 

Chikmagalur –

The coffee land of Karnataka, Chikmagalur is famous for its beautiful hills and sceneries located in the foothills of the Mullayanagiri range.

It is a great attraction for trekkers and those wishing to experience the peaceful ambience of the city.

Major attractions:Mullayanagiri, Baba Budangiri, Rafting in Bhadra River, Kemmangundi, Hebbe falls etc...
