Andhra Pradesh

Situated on the South eastern coast of India, Andhra is the seventh largest state comprising of beautiful dams, valleys, lush green hill stations, tranquil lake waters, swirling beach waves, meadows, resorts, famous temples, caves, virgin forests, sanctuaries and national parks which a visitor can enjoy to his heart's content.

Amaravathi –

Amaravathi ,the capital city of Andhra located on the southern banks of the Krishna river, is a prime hub for pilgrimage,a renowned place for Buddhist relics and sightseeing for tourists.

Thus it is called as theAbode of God.

Major attractions: Amaravathi Museum, Amareswara Temple, Buddhist Stupa, Arch of Harichandrapuram, Krauncha Giri etc...

 

Vishakapatnam –

Vizag or Vishakapatnam, The Jewel of the East Coast offers something for every type of explorer from beaches to cultural sites.

There are eight beaches as well as hilltop parks, a nature preserve, caverns, wildlife sanctuaries, temples, sacred Buddhist monuments and a fascinating submarine museum.

Major attractions: :Borra Caves, INS Kurusura Submarine Museum, Kailasagiri, Araku Valley, Indira Gandhi Zoological Park etc....


 

Vijayawada –

With scrumptious fruits, sensational sweets and scenic marvels, Vijayawada literally translates to 'The Place of Victory'.

Vijayawada holds the credit of being the largest railway junction in South India.

Located on the banks of the Krishna River, It is bounded by the lndrakeeladri Hills on the west and the Budameru river on the north.

Major attractions: Undavalli Caves, Kanaka Durga Temple, Bhavani Island, Prakasam Barrage, Gandhi Hill etc....

 

Rajhamundry –

Rajahmundry is a beautiful city located on the banks of Godavari River, called as the “Born City”.

It has a blend of various qualities that make the place unique, interesting, and suitable for tourism for all ages.

Picturesque landscapes, monuments of cultural importance, ancient temples and adventurous tourist spots are available here.

Major attractions: Papi Hills, Pushkar Ghat, Markandeya Temple, ISKCON Rajahmundry Sri Sri Radha Gopinath Temple, Dowleswaram Barrage, Godavari Boat Travels, Kotilingeswara Temple etc....

 

Chandragiri –

Chandragiri is a village in Chittoor district of the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh with quite a rich history.

Misty hills, evergreen forests, ancient forts, temples, museum etc are some of the attractions here.

Major attractions: Chandragiri Fort, Sri Venkateshwara National Park, Archaelogical Museum etc....
