Tirupathi Special Entry Darshan

The Special Entry Darshan(Seeghra Darshanam) is introduced at Tirupathi Devasthanam to provide quick Darshan to the Pilgrims. Darshan is available in different time slots starting from 10 Am till 9 PM.

We are here to book special entry darshan tickets for you at the cost of Rs 350/- per pilgrims.

You can avail 2 laddu's each with the Seeghra Darshan ticket.


Points to Remember

 Dress code- Traditional Indian wear.

 Children under the age of 12 don't need separate entry tickets.
