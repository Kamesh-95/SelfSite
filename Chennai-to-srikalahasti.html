Srikalahasti Temple

Srikalahasti Temple is located in the town of Srikalahasti, in the state of Andhra Pradesh.

It is the abode of Lord Shiva, from whose head, the Ganges is said to flow and  is said to be the site where Kannappa was ready to offer both his eyes to cover blood flowing from the Siva linga before the Lord Shiva stopped him and granted him mukti.

Sahasra Linga Temple

It is a pilgrimage site famous for 1000 lingas that are carved on the rocks of the river Shalmala and on its banks.

With a forest and hills around, the temple finds itself in a serene background, making a visit to this area all the more fascinating.

 

Talakona Falls

Talakona Falls are situated in Sri Venkateswara National Park, Chittoor district in Andhra Pradesh.

The gorgeous waterfall falling deep into a valley from a height of around 270 feet makes it the highest waterfall in Andhra and also one of the top Tourist places in Andhra Pradesh.

It is believed that the water of this Falls is enriched with herbs with healing powers.


 

Ubbalamadugu Falls

Ubbalamadugu or Tada Falls is a waterfall located in the forest called Siddulaiah Kona, Chittoor district, Andhra Pradesh.

Situated 35 Kms away from Srikalahasti, it is a popular place for trekking, climbing and outing.
