Pondicherry

A former French colony in India until 1954, is now a Union Territory town bounded by Tamil Nadu.

It is one of the best places to get a glimpse of colonial heritage with its lanes having small yellow walled houses along with French cafes serving delicious steaks.

The food, the beer, the beaches, the ashrams, the tranquil in the air, the breezy shores, Pondicherry has a colour for everyone.

Auroville:

Auroville (City of Dawn) is an experimental township in Viluppuram district.

.The idea behind this city is to create a universal town where men and women of all countries are able to live in peace and progressive harmony.

The main attraction here is the "Matrimandir".

With water pooling in from different sides, the sound of the water and cool breeze which always flows, provides a perfect atmosphere for meditation.

 

Sri Aurobindo Ashram

Ashram emphasizes on the principle teachings of yoga for a peaceful and happy life.

It is said that the Mother Goddess manifested herself in the holy Pushkarini calledPadmasarovaram in a golden lotus.

Founded in 1926, the Sri Aurobindo Ashram has grown, under the guidance of the spiritual leader called ‘the Mother’( Mirra Alfassa) from a small group of two dozen disciples into a large diversified spiritual community.


 

Manakula Vinayagar Temple

The vibrant temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha, is known for its elephant blessings, ornately carved stone walls & golden chariot.

In Tamil 'Manal' means sand and 'Kulam' means pond near the sea.

The place which was full of sand near the pond before the temple was built hence people called the god as Manal Kulathu Vinayagar, meaning ‘God near the pond of sand’ and later named as MANAKULA VINAYAGAR.


 

Paradise Beach

Adorned with the golden sand, this famous and highly sought after beach is always swaying with cold sea breeze.

This is a little isolated beach, and to reach here you have to take a ferry across the backwaters through the really green thick mangrove forest.

You can try a bit of fishing and photography here to keep you engaged.
