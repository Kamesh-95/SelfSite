Kerala –God’s Own Country

A state on India's tropical Malabar Coast, flanked by the Arabian Sea in the West, the towering Western Ghats in the East and networked by 44 interconnected rivers, Kerala is gifted with diverse geographical features that have made it one of the best tourist destinations in Asia.

A long shoreline with serene beaches, tranquil stretches of emerald backwaters, lush green hill stations, exotic wildlife awaits you once you step into this paradise.

Munnar –

It is one of the most beautiful and popular hill-stations located in the Idukki district of Kerala.

Situated on the banks of three rivers- Madupetti, Nallathanni and Periavaru, this place is famous for its tea estates, refreshing lakes and abundance of flora and fauna.

Major attractions: The Eravikulam National Park, Salim Ali Bird Sanctuary and tea plantations.

 

Cochin –

Kochi or Cochin is a major port city on the south western coast of India, popularly known as the Queen of Arabian Sea.

It flaunts one of the finest natural harbours of the world. Well known for its beaches, spicy foods and historical places.

To make the most of the enchanting surroundings one can undertake various activities such as trekking, boating, horse riding etc.

Major attractions: : Backwaters of Kochi, Fort Kochi, Hill Palace, Kerala Folkshore Museum, Worshipping places like St.Francis Church, Ernakulam Siva Temple etc……


 

Alleppey –

Alleppey or Alappuzha is the oldest planned backwater town of Kerala.

The city of Alleppey is also known for its beaches, lagoons, temples, boat races, Ayurvedic Spa and Wellness centres.

Major attractions: Houseboat, Vembanad Lake, Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary, Alappuzha Lighthouse, Ambalappuzha Sri Krishna Temple etc……

 

Wayanad –

It is a district in the north-east of Kerala, nestled among the mountains of Western Ghats.

With vast areas still unexplored, Wayanad is truly an adventurous site waiting to be discovered.

. Mountains and forests here create numerous outback tracks, trekking routes and opportunities for other adventure sports.

Major attractions: Banasura Sagar Dam, Edakkal Caves, Kuruva Island, Chembura Peak, Meenmutty Waterfalls etc……

 

Thekkady –

It is a home to the country’s largest Tiger Reserve – Periyar wild life sanctuary, an important tourist attraction in Kerala.

It is a pleasant place on earth for those who love nature in its wild manifestations.

Major attractions: Periyar Tiger Trails, Thekkady Lakes, Bamboo Rafting,Hiking, Nature Walk etc......
